APRIL 2, 2025

IEEPA Tariff Action

The United States imposed a baseline 10% tariff on most imports (excluding Canada and Mexico), and a 125% tariff on imports from China.

ACTIONIEEPA Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIES All countries except Canada and Mexico.
AFFECTED PRODUCTS Most products (see exceptions).
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE) Goods already under Section 232 actions (steel, aluminum, automobiles & parts, pharmaceuticals, semiconductors, certain consumer electronics, copper, lumber, critical minerals) and other items listed in Annex II of E.O. 14257.
TARIFF RATES 10% globally, 125% for China.
STATUS 10% effective April 5th; 125% for China effective April 9th.
REFERENCESSUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
E.O. 14257 Country‑specific tariff rates (11%–50%) are listed in Annex I of the order; those rates were paused for 90 days from 4/10.
90 FR 15509 President increased China rate from 34% to 84%; effective 4/09.
90 FR 15625 Increased the postal‑shipment rate from 90% → 120%.

APRIL 2, 2025

IEEPA Tariff Action

U.S. customs duties on low‑value shipments from China and Hong Kong were reinstated, ending the <$800 de minimis exemption.

ACTIONIEEPA Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIESChina, Hong Kong
AFFECTED PRODUCTS Imports that qualify for de minimis treatment (valued at ≤ $800).
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATES “All applicable duties”—rates vary by product according to the HTSUS.
120% for postal shipments or $150 per postal item.
STATUSEffective April 9th.
REFERENCESSUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
E.O. 14256 E.O. 14256 removed de minimis duty‑free treatment for low‑value imports from China, effective 04/02.
E.O. 14259 Raised the de minimis rate for postal shipments from 30% → 90%.
90 FR 15625 Increased the postal‑shipment rate from 90% → 120%.
