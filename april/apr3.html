APRIL 3, 2025

Retaliatory Tariff Action

China raised tariffs on all U.S. goods in three steps—34 % (Apr 4), 84 % (Apr 9), and finally 125 % (Apr 11).

ACTIONRetaliatory Tariff Action by China
AFFECTED COUNTRIESU.S.
AFFECTED PRODUCTSAll goods from the United States.

TARIFF RATES125 %
