APRIL 15, 2025

Section 232 Tariff Action

The Administration initiated a Section 232 investigation into processed critical minerals, uranium, and derivative products.

ACTION Section 232 Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIES Potentially all countries
AFFECTED PRODUCTS Processed critical minerals, uranium, and derivative products (including semi‑finished and finished products, e.g. semiconductor wafers, electric vehicles, batteries, and smartphones).
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATESTBD.
STATUSAction is currently under investigation.
REFERENCES SUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
E.O. 14272 E.O. 14272 directed the Secretary of Commerce to initiate the investigation on imports of processed minerals and derivative products. (Apr. 15)
