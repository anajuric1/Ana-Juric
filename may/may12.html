MAY 12, 2025

IEEPA Tariff Agreement

The U.S. and China announced a 90‑day tariff reduction deal.

ACTIONIEEPA Tariff Agreement
AFFECTED COUNTRIESChina and U.S.
AFFECTED PRODUCTSAll goods from China.
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATES 10% for items subject to E.O. 14257
20% for items subject to E.O. 14195
10% on U.S. imports into China
10–15% on U.S. imports into China under Tax Commission Announcement No. 1 of 2025 remains.
STATUSN/A.
REFERENCES SUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
U.S. – China Joint Statement White House announces 90‑day reduction of tariffs under E.O. 14257 to 10%; 20% rates under E.O. 14195 remain. China cuts its 125% retaliatory rate to 10% for the period and rolls back non‑tariff actions since Apr. 2.
