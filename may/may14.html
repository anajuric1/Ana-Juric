MAY 14, 2025

IEEPA Tariff Action

The U.S. reduced the Section 232 tariff rate on Chinese imports from 125% to 10% for a 90‑day period under E.O. 14257.

ACTIONIEEPA Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIESChina
AFFECTED PRODUCTSAll goods from China.
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATES10%
STATUSEffective May 14th.
REFERENCESSUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
90 FR 21831 President reduced rate from 125% to 10% on imports from China for a 90‑day period.

MAY 14, 2025

IEEPA Tariff Action

Postal and de minimis shipments from China and Hong Kong are subject to “all applicable duties,” with a specific 54% rate (or $150 per postal item) for the 90‑day period.

ACTIONIEEPA Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIESChina and Hong Kong
AFFECTED PRODUCTS Imports qualifying for de minimis treatment (≤ $800).
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATES "All applicable duties"; rates vary by HTSUS.
54% for postal shipments or $150 per postal item.
STATUSEffective May 14th.
REFERENCESSUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
E.O. 14298 E.O. 14298 reduced the postal rate on de minimis imports from China from 120% to 54% for a 90‑day period.
