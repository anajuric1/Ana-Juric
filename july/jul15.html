JULY 15, 2025

IEEPA Tariff Agreement

The U.S. and Indonesia reached a tariff agreement imposing a 19% rate on Indonesian goods entering the United States. U.S. exports to Indonesia receive a 0% rate. Indonesia also agreed to purchase U.S. aircraft, energy supplies, and agricultural products.
