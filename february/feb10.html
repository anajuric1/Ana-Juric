FEBRUARY 10, 2025

Section 232 Tariff Action

Tariffs expanded to include steel, aluminum, and their derivative products from all countries. Actions taken under Executive Orders 10895 and 10896.

ACTIONSection 232 Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIESAll countries
AFFECTED PRODUCTSSteel, derivative products of steel, aluminum, derivative products of aluminum.
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATE25%
STATUSEffective March 12th.
REFERENCES SUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
E.O. 10896 E.O. 10896 announced the expanded steel tariffs (Feb. 10).
E.O. 10895 E.O. 10896 announced the expanded aluminum tariffs (Feb. 10).
Expanded Section 232 Tariffs on Steel and Aluminum The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) published implementing regulations.

FEBRUARY 10, 2025

Retaliatory Tariff Action

China imposed retaliatory tariffs of 10–15% on select U.S. goods in response to expanded U.S. tariffs.

ACTIONRetaliatory Tariff Action by China
AFFECTED COUNTRIESU.S.
AFFECTED PRODUCTSCoal, liquefied natural gas, crude oil, agricultural machinery, large-displacement cars and pickup trucks.
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATE10–15%
STATUSEffective February 10th.
