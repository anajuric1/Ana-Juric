FEBRUARY 1, 2025

IEEPA Tariff Action

The U.S. imposes tariffs on Canada and Mexico under the IEEPA, targeting a wide range of imported goods with exceptions under the USMCA agreement.

ACTIONIEEPA Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIESCanada, Mexico
AFFECTED PRODUCTSAll goods from Canada and Mexico.
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)Imports that qualify for duty-free preference under USMCA.
TARIFF RATES25% for goods. 10% for energy and potash.
STATUS25% for goods effective March 4th.
10% for energy and potash effective March 7th.
REFERENCES SUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
IEEPA, 50 U.S.C. §§1701 et seq.The International Emergency Economic Powers Act ("IEEPA") is codified under this U.S. Code.
E.O. 14193E.O. 14193 declared the tariff increase for imports from Canada (Feb. 1).
E.O. 14194E.O. 14194 declared the tariff increase for imports from Mexico (Feb. 1).
E.O. 14197E.O. 14197 paused the tariff increase until March 4th (Feb. 3).
E.O. 14231E.O. 14231 added the exception for USMCA preferential treatment qualifying imports and reduced rates for potash from 25% to 10% (Mar. 6).

FEBRUARY 1, 2025

IEEPA Tariff Action

U.S. imposed tariffs on low-value imports (under $800) from China and Hong Kong, removing their prior duty-free status under de minimis rules.

ACTIONIEEPA Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIESChina, Hong Kong
AFFECTED PRODUCTSImports that qualify for de minimis treatment (valued at or under $800).
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATES"All applicable duties"; tariff rates vary by product, HTSUS is to be referenced.
STATUSEffective February 1st.
REFERENCES SUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
19 U.S.C. § 1321(a)(2)(C)This U.S. code identifies de minimis treatment for imports valued at or under $800.
E.O. 14195E.O. 14195 announced the removal of de minimis duty free treatment on low-value imports from China (Feb. 1).
E.O. 14200E.O. 14200 reinstated de minimis duty free treatment on low-value imports from China (Feb. 5).
