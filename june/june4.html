JUNE 4, 2025

Section 232 Tariff Action

Tariffs on steel and aluminum (and their derivative products) were raised from 25% to 50% for all partners except the U.K., which remains at 25%.

ACTIONSection 232 Tariff Action
AFFECTED COUNTRIESAll countries except the U.K.
AFFECTED PRODUCTS Steel and aluminum (and their derivative products).
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE)N/A.
TARIFF RATES50%
STATUSEffective June 4th.
