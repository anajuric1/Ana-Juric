JUNE 16, 2025

Section 232 Tariff Agreement

The U.S. and U.K. reached an agreement setting a lower 10% rate (with a tariff‑rate quota) on certain vehicle imports from the U.K.

ACTIONSection 232 Tariff Agreement
AFFECTED COUNTRIESU.S. and U.K.
AFFECTED PRODUCTSPassenger vehicles, light trucks, and certain automobile parts.
EXCEPTIONS (IF APPLICABLE) Automobile parts entering the U.S. under USMCA. For vehicle imports qualifying for USMCA preference, tariffs may apply only to the non‑U.S. content of the product.
TARIFF RATES10%
STATUSEffective June 30th.
REFERENCESSUPPLEMENTAL NOTES
E.O. 14309 E.O. 14309 announced the U.S.–U.K. agreement and set a 10% tariff‑rate quota for U.K. vehicles.
